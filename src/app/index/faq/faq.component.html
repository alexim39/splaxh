WHAT IS TINOTUNE?

Tinotune is an online initiative that aim is to eliminate the cost associated with promoting the songs of upcoming artistes. Tinotune works with top music artistes from Nigeria.

Their role will be to listen to every song uploaded, make selection, and feature those behind the selected songs on the singles. All the upcoming artistes are required to od is upload their songs on the website, each song uploaded will cost N500.

HOW IT WORKS?
  • Visit the website at www.tinotune.com
  • Click on upload song. Enter your name, email and password to have a profile with us
  • Upload your song (audio or video)
 CAN I UPLOAD SONG WITH MY PHONE?

Yes. The website is designed to adapt to screen size to enable you use any device.
